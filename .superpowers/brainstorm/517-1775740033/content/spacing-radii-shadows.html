shadow-modal modals, drawers, tooltips
shadow-focus focused inputs, selected state

Design rationale: Radii are intentionally restrained — radius-lg at 10px is the maximum for cards. No 16px+ rounding. This keeps Babylon feeling precise and professional rather than playful. Shadows in dark mode rely on black depth, not color. The focus shadow is the only place accent purple bleeds into interaction state.
